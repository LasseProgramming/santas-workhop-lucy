Panic at the North Pole

Oh no, it seems that while Santa was sleeping yesterday, the naughty elves had a secret party and got shitfaced in the eggnog. During their monkey party, the naughty elves had the crazy idea of putting locks on all the presents, so that no kids would be able to open their gift on Christmas, causing all of them to tantrum and thereby starting the apocalypse. The elves were hoping that if the world ended, they never had to make gifts again and could party every night.

Your present is among the locked ones.

Fortunately, there was a few elves that stayed sober. They knew they would be unable to stop the drunken elves, but they managed to write down clues to the combinations of all locks. To ensure the drunken elves did not find and destroy the clues, the elves wrapping the clues as presents. As there are millions of presents in santa's workshop, they were unable to keep track of which presents contained the clues for your present.

It seems the you will have to search through the presents yourself, to find the clues for your present.
